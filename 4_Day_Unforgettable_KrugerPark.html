This safari package takes you on an unforgettable safari experience in the Kruger National Park. Taking you into the heart of the real African bush, bringing you face to face with Africa's wildlife. Including 3 nights accommodation, a sunset and sunrise safari, and two full-day safaris in Kruger Park. All in an open safari vehicle, with a professional nature guide. Dinner and breakfast are included, as well as entrance fees to Kruger Park and a return shuttle from Johannesburg airport.
