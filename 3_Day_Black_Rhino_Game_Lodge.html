Day 1

Johannesburg to Hotel Numbi Transfer & Kruger Park Sunset Safari

Johannesburg to Hotel Numbi Transfer & Kruger Park Sunset Safari

Departure takes place from OR Tambo International airport in Johannesburg by shuttle to Hotel Numbi - A charming country hotel in Hazyview, a short 15 km from Kruger Park, providing the perfect gateway to explore the entire Kruger region, where you will stay for the duration of your safari.

Hotel Numbi offers cozy and comfortable rooms, ensuite with air-conditioner, tea and coffee making facilities and satellite TV. Amenities include swimming pool, bar & two onsite restaurants.

After check-in, we depart in the early afternoon for your first safari in the Kruger National Park, this safari takes you into Kruger in the late afternoon/ early evening when the animals begin to get active again after the mid-day heat, maximizing your viewing potential, youll be able to enjoy the natural environment in an open safari vehicle with a professional nature guide before returning to your accommodation to enjoy an authentic African inspired menu specially prepared by the Hotels local chef.

Day 2-3

Full Day Safari in the Kruger National Park

Full Day Safari in the Kruger National Park

Your Kruger Park Safari experience begins in the early morning, to explore the Kruger Park by open safari vehicle giving you a full 180-degree panoramic view of the African savanna from a high vantage point.

Taking you into the heart of the real African bush, bringing you face to face with Africa's iconic wildlife. Your dedicated professional nature guide will use expert knowledge of the African bush, to interpret the natural environment for you in every detail.

Your guide will frequent various waterholes and river systems as well as other Kruger hotspots to maximize your opportunity to see as much as possible and ensure the scenery on display is ever-changing and picturesque. Breakfast will be individually packed by the lodge chef to be enjoyed at a rest camp or picnic spot, before we continue on safari for the rest of the day, stopping for lunch in between.

We will return to your accommodation in the afternoon before dinner is expertly prepared and served in the restaurant.

Day 4

Morning Safari in Kruger & Transfer Back to Johannesburg

Morning Safari in Kruger & Transfer Back to Johannesburg

On your final day, you will enjoy a sunrise safari in Kruger Park. This safari will also take place in the most animal-rich area of the Kruger, with an expert nature guide in an open safari vehicle, providing an uninterrupted game viewing experience.

It gives you maximum exposure to the Kruger National Park over your 3 days on safari. Breakfast will be prepared and packaged by the hotel to be enjoyed during your safari in the Kruger.

After your sunrise Kruger safari, you will meet your shuttle, which will transfer you safely back to O.R Tambo International Airport in Johannesburg. Departing with a lifetime of amazing memories of an unforgettable Kruger Park safari in the African savanna.
