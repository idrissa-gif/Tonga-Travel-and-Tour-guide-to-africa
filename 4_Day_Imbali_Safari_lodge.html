On this popular tour, you’ll discover the scenic wonders of this unique reserve which lies at the base of an ancient volcanic crater and in a transitional zone between the dry, arid Kalahari Desert and lush Lowveld region. The geology and ecology of the reserve are fascinating, and it’s famous for having the highest concentration of game in the country. On top of that, Greater Pilanesberg is a malaria-free region.

Day 1

Johannesburg - Black Rhino Game Lodge

Johannesburg - Black Rhino Game Lodge

Guests are picked up early in Johannesburg, from the Johannesburg OR Tambo Airport or any Hotel in Johannesburg, and driven to Black Rhino Private Game Reserve in Greater Pilanesberg. Arrive at Black Rhino Game Lodge mid-day after exploring the game reserve for a few hours. Enjoy afternoon tea, snacks and relax before it’s time to depart for a late-afternoon game drive in the Pilanesberg Game Reserve. Game drives are in an open safari vehicle with a professional ranger. Return to the lodge as the sun is setting and enjoy a starlit dinner around the firepit on warm evenings.

Main Destination:
Pilanesberg Game Reserve

Day 2

Open Vehicle Safari Drives

Open Vehicle Safari Drives

Set off early after hot beverages for a 3-hour sunrise game drive in the Pilanesberg Game Reserve. Return to the lodge for breakfast and spend the rest of the day at the pool or in your suite. Enjoy an Afternoon Tea with snacks and relaxation before it’s time to depart for the late-afternoon game drive. End your day with a delicious dinner in the restaurant or at the outdoor boma on warm evenings.

Black Rhino Game Lodge - Johannesburg

Black Rhino Game Lodge - Johannesburg
