Kruger Park

Where nearly 2 million hectares of unrivaled diversity of life forms fuses with historical and archaeological sights - this is real Africa. The world-renowned Kruger National Park offers a wildlife experience that ranks with the best in Africa.

Giza Necrophile

The last surviving wonder of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World, the Pyramids of Giza are the most prime tourist attractions in Egypt. On the outer edge of this magnificent sprawl lies one of the wonders of the ancient world.

Prison Island

Located just off the coast of Unguja, Prison Island is a hidden oasis which has a fascinating history. Famous for its population of Aldabra giant tortoises and ruins of a former prison, Prison Island is a captivating day trip which compliments a wider Zanzibari itinerary.

Cape Town

As far as the best tourist attractions in Africa go, Cape Town as a city and its surrounding area is very much the easiest and most accessible way to get a taste of Africa as a whole.

Victoria Falls

Known in modern terms as the greatest curtain of falling water in the world, Victoria Falls presents a spectacular sight of awe-inspiring beauty and grandeur on the Zambezi River, forming the border between Zambia and Zimbabwe

A beautiful, flat-topped mountain overlooks the South African city of Cape Town. This mountain is a prominent tourist attraction, pulling up to 4.2 million people annually.

Mount Kilimanjaro

In the far north of Tanzania, is located the famed Mount Kilimanjaro. The peak is the tallest in Africa, and the tallest free-standing mountain in the world, making it a bucket-list destination for many travelers.

In Northeast Tanzania is located one of Africa’s most incredible conservation areas. Serengeti means "endless plains", and that is exactly what you’ll find within the park. More than a million wildebeest migrate through the plains each year.
